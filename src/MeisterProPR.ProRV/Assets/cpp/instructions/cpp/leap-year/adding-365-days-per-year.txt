Review focus: Arithmetic operation assumes 365 days per year

What to look for:
- When an arithmetic operation modifies a date by a constant value of 365, it may be a sign that leap years are not taken into account.
- When performing arithmetic operations on a variable that represents a date, leap years must be taken into account. It is not safe to assume that a year is 365 days long.
